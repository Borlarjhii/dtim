Behind-The-Scene

In My Neighborhood-Dallas was launched in October 2020 to produce short films of exemplary creatives, sponsorships from local businesses and features of non-Texans celebrities and/or influencers to experience Dallas arts and culture in a collaborative fashion.

The idea is designed to provide a safe space for rising creatives to connect and advance their artistics mastery, while showcasing the authenticity of Dallas lifstyle and culture in the best picture.
